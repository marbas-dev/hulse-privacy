Hulse — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026
App: Hulse (iOS)
Developer: Magnus Creative
Contact: aytacmetu@gmail.com

Hulse is a single-player reflex game. The game itself runs entirely on your device. The only feature that uses the internet is an optional rewarded ad you can choose to watch to continue a run. This policy explains, plainly, what that involves.

The short version

  • Your best score and your sound/haptics settings are stored only on your device. We never send them anywhere and we cannot see them.
  • Hulse has no account, no login, no sign-up, and no analytics SDK of our own.
  • When you choose to watch a “revive” ad, Google’s advertising service (Google AdMob) processes some data to show that ad. We have configured ads to be non-personalized.
  • We do not use Apple’s advertising identifier (IDFA), we do not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt, and Hulse does not track you across other apps or websites.

Information stored on your device

The following stay on your device, in iOS local storage (UserDefaults), and are never transmitted to us or anyone else:

  • Your highest score.
  • Your sound and haptics preferences.

Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Hulse shows one kind of ad: an optional, rewarded “revive” ad. It appears only if you tap to watch it after a qualifying run, so you can continue from your current score. Declining simply ends the run normally. There are no banner ads, no full-screen ads between rounds, and no forced ads.

To serve these ads, we use the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). When an ad is requested, Google may collect and process:

  • a device identifier (used by the advertising service, not Apple’s IDFA),
  • product-interaction / usage information related to the ad (for example, that an ad was requested, shown, or completed), and
  • diagnostic/crash information to keep the ad service functioning.

We request non-personalized ads only (npa=1 is sent with every ad request). Non-personalized ads are not based on a profile of your past behavior; limited data may still be processed for fraud prevention, frequency capping, and to show and measure the ad. This data is handled by Google as an independent controller under its own policies. We do not receive your personal data from Google, and we do not link any of it to an identity, because Hulse has no accounts.

Google’s handling of this data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy and Google’s advertising terms: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Your consent choices

In regions where consent is required (including the EU/EEA, the UK, and Switzerland), Hulse uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for your consent before the first ad is shown. Where consent is required, you can review or change your choice at any time:

  • Open Hulse → Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent, which re-presents the consent form, or
  • Manage Google’s ad settings at https://adssettings.google.com.

Because ads are non-personalized and tied to no account, there is no advertising profile held by us to reset.

Tracking and the IDFA

Hulse does not track you in the sense defined by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework. We do not access Apple’s advertising identifier (IDFA), we include no AdSupport or AppTrackingTransparency code, and we therefore do not (and will not) present an ATT prompt. Our App Store privacy label reports “Data Used to Track You: No.”

Data retention and deletion

  • On-device data (score, settings) persists until you delete it in-app or remove the app; it is never sent to us.
  • We operate no servers and keep no user database, so there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our side.
  • Data processed by Google to serve ads is retained and deleted according to Google’s policies, linked above.

Children

Hulse contains no objectionable content and no user-generated content, and is rated for general audiences. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content is constrained to a general-audiences rating in our ad provider’s settings. If you believe a child has provided information through the ad service, contact Google via the links above; for any concern about the app, contact us at aytacmetu@gmail.com.

Your rights (GDPR / EEA-UK, and KVKK / Türkiye)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Because Hulse holds no personal data about you on any server:

  • Access/erasure of app data: all app data is on your device under your control; delete the app to erase it.
  • Ad-related data: exercise your rights with Google, the controller for that processing, via the links above; withdraw ad consent at any time using the in-app consent form (see “Your consent choices”).

The legal basis for non-personalized advertising is your consent (collected via UMP where required) and our legitimate interest in offering an optional way to continue a run. For users in Türkiye, this processing and your KVKK rights are addressed on the same basis. To make any request, or to ask a question, email aytacmetu@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If we change how Hulse handles data, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
