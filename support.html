Common questions

  • How do I play? A glowing needle sweeps a ring; tap the instant its tip crosses the bright target arc. Dead-center taps score PERFECT. Mistime it, or let the needle sweep past, and the run ends.
  • Is Hulse free? Yes — unlimited plays, no purchase. When you end a run, you can optionally watch a short ad to revive and keep going. It is never forced.
  • My best score reset. Your best score is stored on your device. Deleting and reinstalling the app clears it.
  • Ad consent (EU/UK): open Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent to review or change your choice.
